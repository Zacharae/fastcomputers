C-3PO's Foibles and Follies

Despite being a protocol droid with a vast knowledge of languages and cultures, C-3PO often finds himself in comedic situations:

  • Getting caught in a net on Endor
  • Accidentally calling Chewbacca a "walking carpet"
  • Having his head swapped with a battle droid during the Battle of Geonosis
  • Getting his wires crossed and insulting Han Solo
  • Mistaking Ewoks for deities and trying to worship them
  • Being dismantled and mistaken for a pile of junk on Cloud City
  • Accidentally activating a battle droid factory on Geonosis
  • Constantly bickering with R2-D2

Despite his mishaps, C-3PO remains a loyal and valuable member of the Rebel Alliance and a beloved character in the Star Wars universe.
