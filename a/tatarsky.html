Alex Tatarsky: The Multifaceted Clown and Actress

Alex Tatarsky is a clown and actress from New York. She began performing as a clown in the late 1990s, and quickly gained a reputation as a talented and innovative performer.

Over the years, Tatarsky has developed a unique style of clowning that combines physical comedy, puppetry, and storytelling. Her performances are often surreal and dreamlike, and explore themes of identity, memory, and the human condition.

In addition to her work as a clown, Tatarsky is also an accomplished actress. She has appeared in numerous plays and films, and has received critical acclaim for her performances.

Tatarsky's work has had a significant impact on the world of clowning and physical theater. She has inspired countless performers with her unique vision and her commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in performance.

Today, Tatarsky continues to perform and teach, and remains a beloved figure in the world of clowning.
