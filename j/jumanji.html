Robin Williams in Jumanji

Robin Williams delivered a brilliant performance in the 1995 movie Jumanji, in which he played Alan Parrish, a man who had been trapped in a board game for 26 years.

Williams' portrayal of Parrish was both heartwarming and hilarious, as he navigated his way through the dangers of the jungle and reconnected with his childhood friend, played by Bonnie Hunt.

Jumanji remains a beloved classic to this day, and Robin Williams' performance is a big reason why.
