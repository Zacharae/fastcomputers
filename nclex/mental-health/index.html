Mental Health Nursing on the NCLEX-RN

The NCLEX-RN exam includes questions on a wide range of nursing topics, including mental health nursing. Below are some of the key topics you should be familiar with when studying for the exam:

  • Therapeutic communication techniques
  • Psychiatric disorders (e.g. schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, depression)
  • Substance abuse and addiction
  • Crisis intervention and management
  • Psychopharmacology
  • Legal and ethical issues related to mental health
  • Assessment and diagnosis of mental health disorders
  • Treatment and care planning for individuals with mental health disorders

These are just a few examples of the topics covered in mental health nursing on the NCLEX-RN exam. Be sure to review your study materials thoroughly to ensure that you are fully prepared for the exam.
