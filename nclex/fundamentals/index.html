Nursing Fundamentals for NCLEX Students

Nursing fundamentals is an essential component of nursing education and practice. It encompasses a wide range of topics, including:

  • Anatomy and physiology
  • Assessment techniques
  • Communication skills
  • Infection control
  • Vital signs measurement
  • Medication administration
  • Nursing process and care planning
  • Patient education
  • Documentation and record-keeping

As a nursing student preparing for the NCLEX exam, it's important to have a strong understanding of nursing fundamentals. Mastery of these concepts will help you provide safe and effective patient care in a variety of healthcare settings.
