Enso Zen Circle

The Enso is a circle that is hand-drawn in one or two uninhibited brushstrokes to express a moment when the mind is free to let the body create. It represents enlightenment, strength, and the universe. The circle is often seen as an emblem of perfection, eternity, and the void. It can be interpreted in many ways, depending on the viewer's perspective and the context in which it is presented.
