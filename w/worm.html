Resources for Microbiology and Parasitic Worms

  • CDC - Parasites

    Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) - Parasites: This is a comprehensive resource on parasitic diseases, including information on transmission, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. It includes a section on parasitic worms and provides detailed information on specific types of worm infections.

  • American Society for Microbiology (ASM)

    American Society for Microbiology (ASM): The ASM is a professional organization for microbiologists, and their website offers a wealth of information on microbiology research, education, and resources. They also publish several peer-reviewed journals that cover all aspects of microbiology.

  • Microbiology Online

    Microbiology Online: This website provides a range of resources and information on microbiology, including tutorials, quizzes, and reference materials. It covers topics such as microbial diversity, microbial physiology, and medical microbiology.

  • WormBase

    WormBase: This is an online database and resource center for the study of nematode worms, including the model organism C. elegans. It includes genetic and genomic data, as well as information on worm anatomy, behavior, and development.
